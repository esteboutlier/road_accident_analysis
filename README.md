## 📌 Project Overview

This repository contains a comprehensive, interactive Excel Data Dashboard designed to analyze historical road safety records for the years 2021 and 2022. The objective of this project is to transform raw accident records into an intuitive visual intelligence tool that allows stakeholders to identify high-risk safety profiles, track seasonal trends, and optimize emergency response allocations.

---

## 💼 Business Context & Requirements

To simulate a real-world analytics scenario, the dashboard was built to fulfill a specific requirement matrix provided by the **Operations Safety Committee**. The core operational needs included:

- **Core KPIs:** A centralized view of total casualties alongside sub-breakdowns of accident severity (Fatal, Serious, Slight) and their respective percentages.
- **Vehicle Segmentations:** Identification of high-risk vehicle types and overall casualty distributions by vehicle category.
- **Temporal & Trend Analysis:** A monthly tracking system comparing Current Year (CY) metrics directly against Previous Year (PY) historical baselines.
- **Environmental Infrastructure:** Visual distributions of casualties segmented by Road Type and Road Surface conditions.
- **Contextual Matrix:** Cross-analysis intersecting geographic locations (Urban vs. Rural) with temporal categories (Day vs. Night).

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Advanced Excel Features Used

- **Data Cleaning & Engineering:** Replace functions, remove duplicates, null treatment, and custom date/time bucketing logic.
- **Aggregation Engine:** Dynamic Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts for multi-dimensional data structuring.
- **UI/UX & Dashboard Design:** Custom KPI Cards, Slicers (Timeline & Category cross-filtering), Conditional Formatting, and cohesive color palettes for maximum scannability.

---

## 🚀 Key Dashboard Features

1. **Interactive Filter Panel:** Seamlessly filter the entire report by specific periods (Years/Quarters) or geographic zones (Urban vs. Rural).
2. **CY vs PY Monthly Trendlines:** Instantly spot seasonal spikes by observing monthly performance tracking across multi-year cycles.
3. **Casualty Metrics At a Glance:** Dynamic donut charts and metric cards highlighting the proportional weight of fatal, serious, and slight accidents.
4. **Contextual Risk Mapping:** Multi-axis comparisons showing how casualties behave across various road surface states (dry, wet, snowy) and lighting conditions.
